About Me


I am a researcher at the university of Naples Federico II. I was awarded a dual award PhD title in 2022 from the University of Naples Federico II and the University of Bristol and I have been post-doc at the University of Naples Federico II and at the Scuola Superiore Meridionale.

My research focuses on the modeling, analysis and control of large-scale complex systems. Specifically, I am interested in how to model, analyse and coordinate these systems to achieve desired collective behaviours. Do you want to know more about my research?
